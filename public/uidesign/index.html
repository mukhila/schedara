FAQ

Questions, answered.

Which networks does Schedara support?
Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Threads, and Pinterest — with native publishing, no browser extensions or "remind me to post" hacks. Bluesky and Mastodon are on the public roadmap for Q3.
How does the AI Caption Studio work?
Train it once on your past top-performing posts and brand guidelines. Then ask it for a month of content in your voice — playful, technical, or anywhere in between. Every output is editable and version-controlled.
Can I import my existing content from Hootsuite or Buffer?
Yes — one-click CSV import preserves your queue, channels, and labels. Our migration team will white-glove the move for any team over 10 seats, at no cost.
Is my data secure?
SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and ISO 27001 certified. Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never train AI models on your content.
What happens after the free trial?
Day 15 your workspace downgrades to the free Starter tier — no charge, no lost data. Upgrade any time. We'll send a reminder on day 12, never a surprise charge.
